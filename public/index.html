Urgent Watch

Israel–Iran: The Twelve-Day War

In June 2025, Israel launched Operation Rising Lion, striking Iranian nuclear and missile sites. Iran retaliated; a ceasefire was mediated by the U.S. in what became known as the Twelve-Day War. Ayatollah Khamenei has since been killed in subsequent strikes. Ancient Persia (Iran) is identified in Ezekiel 38 as a key Gog-Magog nation.

"Persia, Ethiopia, and Libya with them..." — Ezekiel 38:5

⚠ Active Watch

Blood Moon on Purim — March 2026

A total lunar eclipse landed directly on Purim on March 3, 2026 — the third in a recent series. Christian prophecy leaders including Hagee and Biltz have long noted blood moons' historic correlation with pivotal events for Israel. Genesis 1:14 declares the heavens are for "signs and seasons."

"The sun shall be turned into darkness, and the moon into blood, before the great and terrible day of the LORD come." — Joel 2:31

✦ Prophetic Sign

Mass Jewish Interest in Jesus

Reports indicate unprecedented numbers of Jewish people — including Israelis inside Israel — are asking about biblical prophecy, Psalm 83, and the Messiah. This echoes Romans 11 regarding the re-grafting of natural branches, which Paul links to the fullness of the Gentiles coming in.

"And so all Israel shall be saved..." — Romans 11:26

Watch Now

Iran Nuclear Crisis & Global Realignment

The Trump Administration's negotiations with Iran collapsed; military strikes have reshaped the Middle East geopolitical map dramatically in 2025–2026. Nations are realigning at a pace unlike any prior era, echoing Ezekiel 38–39 formations.

"After many days thou shalt be visited... in the latter years..." — Ezekiel 38:8

✦ Prophetic Sign

Global Apostasy & One-World Religion

Religious syncretism is accelerating globally. Major institutions are merging doctrines and diluting the exclusivity of Christ. The great falling away Paul warned of in 2 Thessalonians 2 appears to be intensifying.

"Let no man deceive you by any means: for that day shall not come, except there come a falling away first..." — 2 Thess. 2:3

⚠ Active Watch

Digital Currency & Mark System Infrastructure

Global central bank digital currencies (CBDCs) are in advanced trials across dozens of nations. The technological infrastructure for a global buying-and-selling control system is now fully conceivable for the first time in history.

"...that no man might buy or sell, save he that had the mark..." — Revelation 13:17

✦ Fulfilled

Israel Reborn — May 14, 1948

The nation of Israel was re-established in a single day, fulfilling Isaiah 66:8. This is widely regarded as the "super-sign" of the end times generation. The fig tree has bloomed.

"Who hath heard such a thing? shall a nation be born at once?" — Isaiah 66:8

✦ Fulfilled

Jerusalem Restored — June 7, 1967

Israel recaptured Jerusalem in the Six-Day War, fulfilling Luke 21:24. The times of the Gentiles concerning Jerusalem have entered their final phase.

"Jerusalem shall be trodden down of the Gentiles, until the times of the Gentiles be fulfilled." — Luke 21:24

Unfolding Now

Temple Mount Tensions

Unprecedented activity around the Temple Mount. Jewish groups openly preparing temple vessels and priestly garments. The Sanhedrin has been re-established. Amir Sarfati and Perry Stone have both noted the acceleration of temple preparation activity.

"...and the daily sacrifice shall be taken away, and the abomination that maketh desolate set up..." — Daniel 12:11

⚠ Active Watch

Wars & Rumors of Wars

The Russia-Ukraine war reshaped European security architecture. Middle Eastern conflicts are multiplying. NATO nations are in emergency rearmament mode. Jesus described this as "birth pangs" — and the contractions are intensifying.

"And ye shall hear of wars and rumours of wars... all these are the beginning of sorrows." — Matthew 24:6,8

⚠ Active Watch

Pestilence & Famine Increase

Post-pandemic disruptions, global food shortages, and new disease outbreaks continue to emerge. Jesus listed pestilences and famines alongside wars as birth pang signs preceding His return.

"And there shall be famines, and pestilences, and earthquakes, in divers places." — Matthew 24:7

✦ Sign

Knowledge & Travel Explosion

Daniel prophesied that in the last days, "many shall run to and fro, and knowledge shall be increased." We live in the age of instant global communication, AI, and mass travel — unprecedented in human history.

"...many shall run to and fro, and knowledge shall be increased." — Daniel 12:4

2026 Sign

Blood Moon on Purim — March 3, 2026

The third blood moon in a remarkable series fell exactly on the Jewish feast of Purim, 2026 — the day celebrating Israel's deliverance from ancient Persia (Iran). As modern Iran faced dramatic defeat, heavens spoke.

"And I will shew wonders in the heavens..." — Joel 2:30

⚠ Approaching

2032–2033 Blood Moon Tetrad

A coming tetrad of four blood moons will occur in 2032–2033 — exactly 2,000 years after the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Many prophecy scholars are watching this window with extraordinary attention.

"The sun shall be turned into darkness, and the moon into blood..." — Acts 2:20

✦ Prophetic Sign

Solar Eclipse Signs Over America

Unusual solar and celestial events have occurred with increasing frequency over North America and the Middle East. Many watchmen note these as divine attention-getters — God speaking through His creation (Genesis 1:14).

"...Let them be for signs, and for seasons..." — Genesis 1:14
